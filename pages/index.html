Features

  • 🕐A single hand sweeps a 1–24 dial once a day, like a physical wall-planner clock.
  • 📅Tasks are your Google Calendar events — no separate database, no manual sync.
  • ↔️Today/Tomorrow toggle to preview what's next.
  • 🌍English, German, and Spanish, switchable in Settings.

Screenshot

This app only reads your primary Google Calendar — it's currently read-only, with no in-app way to add, edit, or delete events. Google Sign-In only works for accounts added as a test user on the developer's OAuth consent screen (it's not yet publicly verified) — if sign-in fails, ask the developer to add your Google account.
